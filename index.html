how it works

Craft Your Unique Blend

Choose from a diverse range of coffee beans sourced from various regions. Customize your blend with unique flavors and select the perfect roasting level to match your unique taste.

Craft Flavor, Add Your Style

Adjust your coffee's flavor profile with a convenient slider function. Add unique notes like spices, floral, or citrus to make your blend truly yours.

Personalize Packaging & Checkout

Share the journey that inspired your unique coffee blend and select from art that best speaks to your blend. Your words and images will be printed on the package, along with your signature.

Sip, Snap & Share

Enjoy every crafted sip, scan the QR code for an interactive experience, and share your delightful coffee journey on social media.

Get Featured, Get Rewarded

If your blend becomes a hit, you get featured on our site and earn points for exciting future purchases.
